MISSION STATEMENT

Why we care

Trudy’s Heart Foundation is a registered Nongovernmental Organization based in Takoradi, Ghana. Its mission is to provide basic human necessities and, if possible, life’s minor luxuries to those who are less than fortunate. Our greatest focus is on providing for the needs of orphans and forgotten children in Takoradi, Ghana and the surrounding areas.

HOW GENEROUS PEOPLE LIKE YOU CAN CONTRIBUTE

We are enabled to accomplish our mission by acquiring donations of both monetary and material items. Although the best and easiest donations are monetary in nature, the ability to physically hand out some much needed supplies to a struggling family, or a toy to a young distressed orphan provides an instant joy to the needy and a blessing to the donor. A residual positive effect occurs when a donor is able to physically see his or her donation help those in need, by way of pictures and videos.

WHAT TO DONATE

If your'e wondering the types of items you can donate, we believe the best place to start is with some everyday items that we tend to take for granted. Items such as shoes, clothes, toys, school supplies, laptops, and items of the like.
